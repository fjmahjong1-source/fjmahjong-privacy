## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open FJ Mahjong, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Sign in with the same Apple ID you used to make the original purchase. Your full game access will be restored automatically.

### Do I need an internet connection to play?

No. FJ Mahjong works completely offline. The only time an internet connection is needed is during your initial purchase and when restoring purchases.

### How does the free trial work?

You can play the full game for 7 days for free. After the trial ends, a one-time $9.99 purchase unlocks the game forever — no subscriptions, no ads, no hidden fees.

### How do I switch between Classic Green and Lunar Red themes?

Open the in-app settings to choose your preferred theme. You can switch between them at any time.

### I'm having a technical problem.

Please email **fjmahjong1@gmail.com** with a description of the issue and the device you're playing on. Including a screenshot helps us help you faster.

### Where can I learn the rules of Fujian mahjong?

The app includes a built-in "How to Play" section that walks you through the basics. The game itself is designed to be intuitive — most players pick it up within a few hands.
