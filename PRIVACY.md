# Privacy Policy for FJ Mahjong

**Effective Date:** May 9, 2026

This privacy policy explains how the FJ Mahjong app for iPhone and iPad (the "App") handles information. The App is a single-player Mahjong game and is built to collect as little data as possible. There are no servers operated by the developer, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no user accounts.

## Information stored on your device

The App stores the following data locally on your device, using Apple's standard `UserDefaults` storage:

- Your in-game statistics (e.g. wins, current scores, settings).
- Your selected avatar.
- The start date of your free trial.
- A counter used internally to decide when to ask you to rate the App.

This information stays on your device and is never sent to the developer or any third party.

## Information synced via iCloud

The App uses Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store to sync **only the start date of your free trial** across devices signed in to the same Apple ID. This lets your free trial period carry over if you reinstall the App or switch devices. The data is encrypted and managed entirely by Apple — the developer cannot access your iCloud account or its contents.

You can disable iCloud sync at any time from your device's Settings.

## Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock the full game. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. The developer never receives your payment information, Apple ID, name, or address. Apple shares only an anonymous transaction record so that the App can recognise that you have unlocked the full version.

Apple's privacy policy applies to App Store transactions and is available at <https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>.

## App Store ratings

The App may, on rare occasions, show Apple's standard "Rate This App" prompt after several wins. If you choose to leave a rating, that rating is sent to Apple — not to the developer. The developer cannot tie your rating to your identity.

## Contact email

The App includes a "Contact Support" button that opens your email client with a pre-filled subject line. If you choose to send the email, you are voluntarily sharing your email address and the contents of your message with the developer at **fjmahjong1@gmail.com**. This information is used only to respond to your message and is not shared with anyone else.

If you do not tap "Send" in your email client, no information leaves your device.

## Information the App does not collect

The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

- Your name, email address, phone number, or postal address (except via the optional "Contact Support" email above, which you control entirely).
- Your location.
- Your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, calendar, or any other device data.
- Your IP address — the developer operates no server that could log it.
- Analytics, crash reports, or telemetry.
- Advertising identifiers (the App does not contain any advertising SDKs).

The App contains no third-party SDKs that perform tracking.

## Children

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has shared personal information with the developer (for example, by sending an email through the Contact Support button), please contact **fjmahjong1@gmail.com** and the developer will delete the message.

## Your rights

Because the developer does not collect personal information about you through the App, there is generally no personal data to access, export, or delete. If you have any questions or requests regarding your data, you may contact the developer at the address below.

## Changes to this policy
